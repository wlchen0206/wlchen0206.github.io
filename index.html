I am a research assistant at MiuLab, National Taiwan University advised by Prof. Yun-Nung (Vivian) Chen. Prior to my RAship, I obtained a MS degree in Computer Science at National Taiwan University advised by Prof. Hsin-Hsi Chen and affiliated with NLPLab, and I obtained a BS degree in Computer Science at National Yang Ming Chiao Tung University. I was a research intern at AIST advised by Dr. Chung-Chi Chen and affiliated with the Knowledge and Information Research Team. Previously, I was a language ambassador of Cohere For AI's AYA project for Traditional Chinese.

Currently, my research interests broadly lie in trustworthy AI and human-AI alignments in the era of LLM. In partiucular, I have worked on metigating hallucination in text generation, free-text rationales and eliciting zero-shot and expert reasoning capabilities in LLMs.

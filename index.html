I am a first -> second-year CS PhD student at University of Virginia, advised by Prof. Yu Meng. I am fortunate to be supported by UVA Provost's Fellowship and UVA Computer Science Scholar Fellowship.

Previously, I was a research assistant at MiuLab, National Taiwan University, advised by Prof. Yun-Nung (Vivian) Chen. I obtained a MS in Computer Science at National Taiwan University, advised by Prof. Hsin-Hsi Chen and a BS in Computer Science at National Yang Ming Chiao Tung University.

Nowadays, I am interested in (1) scaling test-time compute with generative verifiers, (2) building small yet performant LMs, and (3) improving reliability of LLM-based evaluation.

Excited to be starting my Student Researcher internship at Google MTV! Looking forward to meeting new friends and reconnecting with old ones!

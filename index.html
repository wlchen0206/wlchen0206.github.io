About

I am a research assistent at MiuLab, National Taiwan University advised by Prof. Yun-Nung (Vivian) Chen. Prior to my RAship, I obtained a MS degree in Computer Science at National Taiwan University advised by Prof. Hsin-Hsi Chen and affiliated with NLPLab. I was a research intern at AIST advised by Dr. Chung-Chi Chen and affiliated with the Knowledge and Information Research Team.

Currently, my research interests broadly lie in human-AI interactions in the era of LLM. I am particularly interested in bridging the gap between data-/model- centric approaches and human-centric approaches [4][2] for LLMs. I also work on the metigating hallucination in text generation [5] and free-text rationales [3][1]. Previously, I was also a language ambassador of Cohere For AI's AYA project for Traditional Chinese.
